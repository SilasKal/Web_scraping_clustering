BEFORE YOU START - Application tips




Know exactly which graduate program(s) you wish to apply for
Turn off your browser's pop-up blocker (Chrome, Firefox )
Don't use the "Back" button of your web browser
Be sure to click Save and Exit if you need to leave your application for any reason
Have relevant digital files or scanned documents ready to be uploaded (Please use Chrome or Firefox)

Have PDF copies of your transcripts and other supporting documents ready to be uploaded
Have a valid credit card or debit card available to pay the application fee of $100.00 CAD. The application fee is non-refundable and cannot be waived.
 ($17 CAD for Independent graduate students)
Please note: we accept the following credit cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, JCB, Diners Club, and UATP.















Step-by-step application instructionsFollow our detailed instructions for each step of the application process.See the instructions







Start a new application



You will begin by creating a Concordia Netname account. You can use your Netname to log back into the application if you need more than one session to complete it. We recommend you keep your email open in a separate tab or window to make the account verification process easier.
 



Don't have a Netname?Start a new application


Already have a Netname?Start a new application here










Continue an application



If you have a Netname and have started an application, here you can complete your application and pay the $100.00 CAD application fee.



Pick up where you left off



 If you have already paid your application fee, you can upload documents in the My Student Centre (log-in required).
