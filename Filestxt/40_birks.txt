BIRKS STUDENT SERVICE CENTREYour gateway to Concordia




We are available to assist you remotely or in person.Drop by Monday to Friday from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. Call us at 514-848-2424, ext. 2668 Monday to Thursday from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. orFriday from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. or, email us at students@concordia.ca.




Consult the list of services offered

How we can helpIf you have a block on your account, questions about a university policy or procedure, are unsure where to turn for your situation, or have any other questions, start with us. We’ll help you to understand the matter and make a plan to resolve it.
